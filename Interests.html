MY INTERESTS

PC Technology & Custom Builds

I have a massive passion for Personal Computers. I love exploring the latest hardware components, from powerful GPUs to the latest processors. Building and troubleshooting PCs is not just a hobby, but a way for me to understand how modern technology works from the inside out.

Smartphones & Mobile Innovation

The evolution of smartphones always fascinates me. I enjoy keeping up with the latest mobile technologies, software updates (OS), and hardware innovations. Comparing different flagship devices and understanding their unique features is something I do in my free time.

Automotive & Car

Beyond computers and smartphones, I have a deep interest in the automotive world. I enjoy learning about car engineering, performance modifications, and the latest design trends in the industry. Whether it's a classic or a modern supercar, the combination of speed, power, and aesthetics never fails to capture my attention.

Video Games & Consoles

Gaming is my favorite way to relax. I am fascinated by the evolution of gaming consoles, from classic systems to high-performance hardware. I am interested in many genres including action, adventure, and horror.
